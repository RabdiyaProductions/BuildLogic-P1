The problem P1 solves

Messy tender packs

Drawings, specs, emails and “latest revisions” dumped in a folder with no consistent way to interrogate them – so gaps only appear after order.

Hidden commercial exposure

Ambiguous scope, exclusions, missing prelims and half-defined responsibilities that quietly become variations, EOT claims and disputes later.

No repeatable process

Every QS reviews tenders their own way. Knowledge is in people’s heads, not a system, so you can’t scale or improve it.

What BuildLogic P1 gives you

Structured review checklist

A single, standard review flow – drawings, specs, prelims, programme, interfaces, exclusions and assumptions all captured in one pass.

Commercial risk log

Flag gaps, grey areas and misalignments clearly so they can be priced, clarified or negotiated before you issue the order.

Export for sign-off

Generate a clean summary you can attach to internal approvals and client discussions, giving a defensible record of what was agreed.

Who it’s built for

Main contractors, developers and subcontractors running live work who are done with “we thought that was included” conversations.

  • Senior QS and Commercial Managers on complex jobs.
  • Project Managers who need a clear basis of order.
  • Subcontractors who want to lock down scope and avoid being the unofficial contingency.
